№ 05The three greens

Mint is the glow.
Seafoam, the air.
Emerald, the action.

The glow

Mint

The halo around the primary action. The soft shadow under the button. Never a surface.

The air

Seafoam

Atmospheric washes. Aurora halos. Hero backgrounds. Never a fill.

The action

Emerald

The fill of the single primary CTA on every screen. One per view.

№ 09Type · the pair

Aa

Fraunces
Display · Free · Google Fonts

Warm, characterful, optically-sized. At large sizes it shows soft expressive details. At UI sizes it stays clean. The Apple-Music-storytelling vibe. Italics carry the brand's emotional voice.

Geist
Body · Free · Google Fonts

Quiet, modern, neutral. All UI, body, captions. Geist Mono for dates, durations, technical metadata.

№ 14The rule book

If you remember
nothing else, remember these.

Always

Ivory on at least 70% of every screen.
One Emerald CTA per view.
Italics for emotional moments.
Fraunces. Geist. Geist Mono for dates.

Never

Pure white as a page background.
Em dashes in copy. Periods or commas.
Emoji, sparkles, AI-startup gradients.
Two CTAs in the same eye-line.
