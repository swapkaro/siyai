FROM THE EDITOR A note,
for the issue Issue n° 14 · winter 2026

The films on this page were not made by us.

They were made by a son in Bengaluru for his father in Patna, by a daughter in Toronto for the kitchen she misses, by a wife in Dubai for the eleventh year that no one was sure they’d see.

We just keep the projector. The projector is quiet.

There’s a thing we’ve been thinking about, watching these pieces come in. The films that hold are not the elaborate ones. They’re the ones where the person making them knew the smallest thing about the person they were making them for. A particular chai cup. A particular Tuesday. A particular way the radio was always on, but quietly.

If you’re looking to make something for someone, that’s the only advice we have. Don’t reach for the big moment. Reach for the small, specific one. The big moment will follow.

With care, the folks at SiyAI.
