How it works

Three quiet steps. One small wonder.

01

Tell us a moment.

A morning, a meeting, the smell of a house. Speak it, type it, drop a photo. Whatever feels easiest.

02

SiyAI listens.

No prompting, no directing. We read the feeling underneath the words. An army of filmmakers, musicians and writers. Working quietly, for you.

03

A story arrives.

A film, a song, an immersive piece. Yours, alone. Keep it. Share it. Make a gift of it. Leave it for someone you love.
