№ 04The colour philosophy

Colour enters as
light, not paint.

Seventy to eighty percent of any screen is Ivory. Colour is never a fill of the room. It is the lamp that's on, the light spilling from a window, the moment that catches your eye. If a screen feels coloured, we have done it wrong.

№ 09Type · the pair

The voice
is the type.

Aa

Fraunces
Display · Free · Google Fonts

Warm, characterful, optically-sized. At large sizes it shows soft expressive details. At UI sizes it stays clean. The Apple-Music-storytelling vibe. Italics carry the brand's emotional voice.

Geist
Body · Free · Google Fonts

Quiet, modern, neutral. All UI, body, captions. Geist Mono for dates, durations, technical metadata.

№ 14The rule book

If you remember
nothing else, remember these.

Always

Ivory on at least 70% of every screen.
One Emerald CTA per view.
Italics for emotional moments. Always.
Fraunces serif. Geist sans. Geist Mono for dates.

Never

Pure white as a page background.
Em dashes in copy. Use periods or commas.
Emoji, sparkles, or "AI-startup" gradients.
Two CTAs in the same eye-line.
