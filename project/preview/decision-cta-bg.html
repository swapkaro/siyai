Question 02 · Background

Ivory, or pure white?

Same screen. One on Ivory #FFFDF7, one on Pure White #FFFFFF. The difference is small but emotional.

Recommended

A new place for memory
Ivory · #FFFDF7
Warm white · 1.5° toward yellow
Reads as paper, morning light, a gallery. The slight warmth holds hands with serif type and human imagery. Premium brands almost never use pure white.
Cold
Where stories
come alive.
A new place for memory
Pure White · #FFFFFF
Neutral · clinical
Cold. Software-y. Saps the warmth out of the serif and turns "cinematic" into "interface." This is what Notion, Linear, Stripe look like. Not what SiyAI should look like.
Verdict · Background
Ivory, every time. Pure white is allowed only inside elevated cards on ivory. To suggest a sheet of paper lifted from the desk.
