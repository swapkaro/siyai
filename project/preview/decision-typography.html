Type for 16–50.

No prompts · no dashboard · service, not tool
The constraint

One typeface, two kinds of reader.

Because SiyAI has no editing UI. Users share a thought and a story is delivered. The brand is closer to Apple Music storytelling, Letterboxd, Spotify Daylist than to Notion or Linear. Type should feel like the opening of a film, not the chrome of an app. It must also span sixteen to fifty without alienating either end.

16 to 25

Wants Confidence, modernity, slight expression.
Lives in TikTok, Letterboxd, Spotify, Substack. Allergic to "stuffy magazine."

30 to 50

Wants Refinement, calm, legibility.
Reads The Atlantic, listens to Apple Music storytelling pages. Allergic to "trendy."
